Is this Kanban board free to use?

Yes, all core features of our Kanban board, including AI suggestions and collaboration, are completely free.

How does the collaboration feature work?

Simply click the "Share Board" button to generate a unique link. Anyone with the link can view your board in real-time. To edit, they can create their own board.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. Your data is stored securely in your own private database, powered by Google's Firebase. Only you can edit your board, and only people you share the link with can view it.
